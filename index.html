I am an applied mathematician at the University of St Andrews, conducting research in solar magnetohydrodynamics. In particular, my major areas of research are:

  • solar coronal heating via nanoflares;
  • the evolution of the Sun's global magnetic field;
  • diagnosing the nature of 3D magnetic reconnection.
